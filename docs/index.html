Pokémon Regions

I really like Pokémon. In fact, I've been a Pokémon fan since I was 9 years old and played Pokémon Black 2 for the first time. One of my favorite aspects of the Pokémon franchise is how it takes influences from real life. Did you know that every region in the Pokémon universe is based on a different real-world location? The first four generations of main-line Pokémon games take place in fantastical versions of real-life regions of Japan, and every generation after that has taken great inspiration from various locations in both the United States and Europe. You can find out more about this here. In each of these games, the environment, Pokémon designs, characters, and story are interwoven with the culture of the real-world locations they take inspiration from. The tenth generation, Pokémon Winds and Pokémon Waves, will seemingly take place in Southeast Asia. Check it out here!
